Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 18, 2026

DrinkingBuddy ("we," "our," or "the app") is a social blood alcohol content (BAC) estimation tool. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Account information: Username, email address, and full name
  • Profile picture: Optional photo you upload
  • Physical attributes: Weight, height, age, and biological sex (used locally for BAC calculations)

When you use the app, we also process:

  • Drinking session data: Drink type, size, time logged, and estimated BAC
  • Social data: Friends list, group memberships, and group chat messages
  • Device tokens: For delivering push notifications

How We Use Your Information

  • To estimate your BAC based on the Widmark formula
  • To allow friends and group members to see your current BAC
  • To send push notifications (friend requests, group messages)
  • To display your profile to other users in search results and groups

Data Storage

Your data is stored using Google Firebase, which provides industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest. Physical attributes (weight, height, age) used for BAC calculations are stored locally on your device and are not uploaded to our servers.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. Your data is only visible to:

  • Your friends: Can see your username, name, profile picture, and current BAC
  • Group members: Can see your username, name, profile picture, current BAC, and messages you send
  • All users: Can find your username and name via search

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Drinking session data resets automatically when your estimated BAC returns to zero. Session history is stored locally on your device.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Access your data: View your profile and session history within the app
  • Delete your account: Use the "Delete Account" option in Settings to permanently remove all your data from our servers, including your profile, friendships, group memberships, and uploaded photos
  • Clear history: Delete your local session history at any time from Settings

Children's Privacy

DrinkingBuddy is intended for users who are of legal drinking age in their jurisdiction. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under the age of 21 in the United States or under the legal drinking age in other jurisdictions.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
